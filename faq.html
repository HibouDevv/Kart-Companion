FAQ

General Questions

What is Kart Companion? ▼
Kart Companion is a comprehensive statistics tracking and visualization tool for Smash Karts players. It helps you analyze your gameplay, track your progress, and compare your performance across different game modes.
How do I get started with Kart Companion? ▼
Getting started is easy! Simply play then visit any of our stat tracking pages. The system will automatically start tracking your matches and displaying your statistics.
What are the different game modes available? ▼
We track statistics for three main game modes: 3 Minute Mode, Special Mode, and Custom Mode. Each mode has its own dedicated page with specific statistics and visualizations.

Stats and Data

How are my stats calculated? ▼
Our system automatically tracks your in-game performance, including kills, deaths, wins, and other key metrics. These are then processed and displayed in various formats, from detailed numbers to interactive charts.
What do the different statistics mean? ▼
Each statistic represents a different aspect of your gameplay. For example, K/D ratio shows your kill-to-death ratio, while win rate indicates your success in matches. Hover over any statistic for a detailed explanation.
How often is my data updated? ▼
Your statistics are updated in real-time as you play. Each match is automatically recorded and reflected in your stats immediately after completion.

Game Modes and Stats

What is 3 Minute Mode? ▼
3 Minute Mode refers to the standard 12-player, 3-minute duration matches in Smash Karts. These are the regular public lobbies where players compete in fast-paced matches with a time limit of 3 minutes.
What is Special Mode? ▼
Special Mode includes various game types in public lobbies, such as 8 player lobbies, Arms Race, Score Target, Capture the Flag (CTF), Gem Collector, Hat Holder, and Team matches. Each of these modes has unique objectives and gameplay mechanics.
What is Custom Mode? ▼
Custom Mode encompasses any game that is created by users, whether you join someone else's custom game or create your own. These games can have custom rules, settings, and player counts, and are separate from the standard public lobbies.

Player Card

What is a Smash Kart Card? ▼
Your Smash Kart Card is a personalized profile that showcases your key statistics and achievements. It's like a digital trading card that represents your gaming prowess!
How do I customize my player card? ▼
Visit the "My Smash Kart Card" page to customize your card. You can choose which statistics to display, select different themes, and arrange elements to create your unique card.
Can I share my player card with others? ▼
Yes! Your player card can be shared with friends and on social media. Simply use the share button on your card to generate a link or image.

Technical Questions

What browsers are supported? ▼
Kart Companion works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No account is required! Simply log onto your Smash Karts account and the stats will be tracked according to your SKID.
How do I report bugs or issues? ▼
If you encounter any issues, please visit our About page and use the contact form to report the problem. Our team will investigate and respond as soon as possible.
