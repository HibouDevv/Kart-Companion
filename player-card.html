Stats Computation

ATK (Attack)

Based on your average kills per 3 minutes. 20 kills per 3 minutes = 100 ATK.

DEF (Defense)

Based on your average deaths per 3 minutes. 0 deaths = 100 DEF, 16+ deaths = 0 DEF.

CNS (Consistency)

Based on your match completion rate. Higher percentage of completed matches = higher CNS.

EXP (Experience)

Based on your total playtime. Uses a logarithmic scale to reward both new and veteran players.

PRF (Performance)

Based on your overall KDR (Kill/Death Ratio). KDR of 3.50 or higher = 100 PRF.
